About

Our Story

Cybrix Technology was originally founded in 2010 in Dubai, UAE under the name Purple Ocean LLC.

Cybrix Technology is a software development company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We support the growth of MSMEs in Africa through the provision of affordable, high quality and innovative business management technology solutions. We specialize in Software as a Service (SAAS) solutions for retail and hospitality industries.

We focus on providing cutting edge services at affordable costs enabling MSMEs in Africa to focus less time on managing their operations and more time focusing on their most valuable asset - the customer.

What we do

CYBRIX Technology offers business management technology solutions. We specialize in Software as a Service (SaaS) solutions for retail and hospitality industries. Our mission is to provide cutting edge services at affordable costs enabling businesses to focus less time on managing their operations and more time focusing on their most valuable asset - the customer.

Our range of business management technology solutions include:

  • Cybrix ERP (Point of Sale, Bookkeeping, Inventory Managment, HR Management)
  • Cybrix Hardware (POS Station, Receipt Printers, Barcode Scanners)
  • Cybrix Restaurant (in Development)
  • Cybrix Delivery (in Development)
